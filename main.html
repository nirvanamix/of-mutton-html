Welcome to the Shoulder of Mutton

Welcome to the Shoulder of Mutton, an old public house steeped in history - the perfect home for our fantastic range of quality original antiques and collectibles. This family business is run by husband and wife team Jacki and Jon Neil. From furniture to suit all tastes, to crockery for the collector and traditional antique jewellery we have new pieces arriving everyday.

Why not pop in for a browse and settle down to enjoy the surroundings with a drink and something delicous made by Sophie across at our Licensed Vintage Tea Rooms.
