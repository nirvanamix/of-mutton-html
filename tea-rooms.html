Our Licensed Vintage Tea Rooms is run and owned by Sophie Steel and her family.

With over 35 years in the catering industry, between them all, the family has provided catering for royal shows, game fairs, steam rallies, and many private and public events.

Sophie has always had a passion for cooking, particularly baking. At the age of 17, she worked for Linden Lady making and decorating the chocolates and fudge. With the ambition to run her own business, Sophie took on a new challenge and began to run her cafe in Earls Colne. After building the business, outgrowing the premises, and a move to Great Bentley - the next step was to take on the Vintage Tea Rooms at the Shoulder of Mutton.

"We live a mile away and have always visited when the Shoulder of Mutton was a pub, for family dinners, so it all seemed perfect. We are very excited to be working with both Jon and Jackie in this joint venture and look forward to welcoming you all".

Sophie

You can enjoy breakfast, lunch, and afternoon teas in the delightful Tea Rooms and Gardens, or just pop in for a cuppa or a lovely cold glass of something refreshing.

All produce is local, there are vegetarian and vegan menu options and those with allergies or intolerances can also be catered for, you can also take away - take a look at the current menus:
